Why do you need independent valuation?

Independent valuation is often necessary when carrying out any actions related to property. The most common situations when independent expert valuation is required are:

  • transactions for the purchase and sale of residential, commercial real estate, land plots and other types of property;
  • registration of inheritance or donation of real estate;
  • valuation of contribution to authorized capital;
  • property and legal disputes;
  • making management and investment decisions;
  • determining rental rates;
  • lending against collateral;
  • revaluation of assets;
  • implementation of construction or reconstruction projects of real estate objects.
