Partners

"Independent Center of Expertise and Appraisal" actively participates in the development of the consulting services market and the institution of professional valuation. In its activities, the company cooperates with the following organizations and institutions:

Association of Legal Entities "Kyrgyz Appraisers Association"

Professional association of appraisers in Kyrgyzstan, establishing standards and norms in the field of property valuation.

"Union of Kyrgyz Appraisers"

Public organization uniting professional appraisers to protect their interests and develop the profession.

State Committee of the Kyrgyz Republic for State Property Management

State body responsible for managing state property and conducting valuation of state assets.

Municipal Property Management Department of Bishkek City Hall

Local self-government body managing municipal property of the capital of Kyrgyzstan.

European Bank for Reconstruction and Development

International financial institution investing in projects that promote the transition to an open market economy.

Institute of Management Consultants

Professional organization involved in the development and standardization of consulting services in the field of management.
