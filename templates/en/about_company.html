The main activities of the company are:

  • Valuation of all types of property, including intellectual property valuation;
  • Consulting and training in the field of marketing and management.

"Independent Center of Expertise and Appraisal" has been in the consulting and valuation services market since 2001. The company operates in the field of valuation of all types of property, business, intellectual property, as well as investment projects. The company also provides consulting services, marketing and analytical research, strategic business planning, inventory and management strategy development. An extensive network of partners and an established client base enable the company to quickly and effectively solve various tasks. We actively work with both local and foreign commercial companies, as well as with international and donor organizations.

Mission

To contribute to the economic development and success of the client by providing highly qualified and effective solutions. We reveal the potential of companies for their successful development.

Principles

In our company, we adhere to three fundamental principles:

Value

When working with a client, we strive to create value for them, which they use to achieve success.

Excellence

When creating value for the client, we set ourselves challenging tasks. Making optimal decisions, we strive to perform one hundred percent work.

Customer Approach

We always strive for a customer-oriented approach and reject anything that may cause inconvenience to the client.
